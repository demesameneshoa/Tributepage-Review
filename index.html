Here's a time-line of Pele's life:


  • 1940 - Edson Arantes do Nascimento was born on October 23, 1940, in Três Corações, Brazil.

  • 1956 - Pele began playing for Santos Football Club at the age of 16.

  • 1958 - Pele scored two goals in the World Cup final against Sweden, helping Brazil win their first World Cup.

  • 1962 - Pele suffered an injury in the second match of the World Cup but recovered to score four goals in the tournament, helping Brazil win their second World Cup.

  • 1970 - Pele led Brazil to their third World Cup victory, scoring four goals in the tournament, including one in the final against Italy.

  • 1974 - Pele retired from international football.

  • 1975 - Pele played his last match for Santos Football Club.

  • 1999 - Pele was named FIFA's "Player of the Century".

  • 2021 - Pele turned 80 years old.
